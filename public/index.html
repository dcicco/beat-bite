About

Beat-Bite is an interactive web-based music maker! With the freedom to replace notes in a sequence from a variety of different instruments. Saving and sharing custom made beats is easy and allows you to show the world your creation. With the help of the WebAudio API and the Tone.JS library we are able to provide a high quality native experience.
