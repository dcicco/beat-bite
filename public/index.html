About

Beat-Bite is an interactive web-based music maker! With the freedom to place notes in a sequence from a variety of different instruments. Saving and sharing custom made beats is easy and allows you to show the world your creation. With the help of the WebAudio API and Tone.JS we are able to provide a high quality native audio experience. Fabric.JS allows us to manipulate the canvas quick and efficiently with the added bonus of hit detection.

Created by Dante, Justin, Brent, and Jacob.
